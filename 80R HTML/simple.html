CHIKEN FRY

Ingredients

  • chiken
  • chilli powder
  • termaric powder
  • chiken masala
  • oil
  • spices
  • onions
  • ginger garlic paste
  • salt

    • Instructions

    1. Take a bowl and keep it on cylinder flame
    2. pour required amount of oil
    3. add spices and onions cook untill onions comes in golden brown color
    4. add ginger garlic paste and termeric powder
    5. add chiken and cook 5 mins
    6. after 5 mins add chilli powder and salt cook for 30 mins
    7. after 30 mins add chiken masala and cook for 5 mins
    8. chiken fry is ready
