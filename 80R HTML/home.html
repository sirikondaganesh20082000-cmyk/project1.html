About Sankranti

Sankranti is a popular harvest festival celebrated across India. It marks the transition of the Sun into the zodiac sign Capricorn.

Main Rituals

  1. Bhogi Celebration
  2. Surya Puja
  3. Kite Flying
  4. Traditional Rangoli

Traditional Foods

  • Pongal
  • Tilgul
  • Ariselu
  • Sweet Rice
